About COHORT' 24

The Association of Computer Intelligence and Design (ACID), the Student Association of the Department of Computer Science and Business Systems is thrilled to present and conduct "COHORT '24", A National level technical Symposium, on February 21st, 2024 at Knowledge Institute Of Technology. "COHORT '24" aims to bring the brightest minds and budding Engineers to showcase their stalwart of knowledge. Come together, To unleash the talents!

Department of
Computer Science & Business Systems

Vision

The Department of CSBS was established in the year 2020. The department offers U.G. Degree Programme in B.Tech Computer Science and Business Systems. It is strengthened by it's experienced and dedicated faculty members from recognized Universities and organizations. The aim of the department is to produce globally and socially sensitized engineering graduates to bring out quality in the frontier areas of Computer Science and Business Systems.

To impart technical knowledge through innovative teaching, research and consultancy, to adapt the dynamic needs of industries through curriculum update. To produce competent engineers with professionally enhanced and employability skills.To pursue global standards of excellence in all our endeavors namely teaching, research, consultancy, continuing education and support functions.

Mission

The future demands of the IT industry whose landscape is rapidly changing in the era of Business 4.0. The curriculum is designed to ensure that students should get better exposure to emerging subjects such as Analytics, Deep learning, Machine Learning, Mobile Computing, Cloud Computing, Internet of Things and Design thinking to develop an equal interest and appreciation towards humanity, value added science and other interrelated topics.

The students graduating from this program will be in high demand by the industries and deal with creation, management, business and maintenance of the system includes software, hardware, artificial intelligence, data mining and Information Security. The prominent companies such as TCS, Amazon, Atos | Syntel, ZOHO, Infosys, Cognizant, Virtusa, Aspire Systems, Wipro and Amphisoft visit our campus and offer good placements.
